Apple pie dumplings receipe

  • sliced apples
  • cinnamon
  • ready-made pie crust
  • egg yolk
  • brown sugar

1. Alice apples into cubes
2. put apple cubes into bowl, add brown sugar
3. Mix until gooey
4. put apple cub mix in the middle of pie crust
5. Crack egg in seperate brwon and stir (egg yolk for edges of pie crust to seal it )
6. Fold into dumpling shape
7. Add edge texture with anad stir in middle (if you don't, the dumplings will explode )
8. Bake in oven
9. Wait to cool, spread surface with egg yolk
10. We're done!
